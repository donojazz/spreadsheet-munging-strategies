2 Tidy tables with gotchas

This part is a gentle introduction, by taking what you already know about importing tidy tabular data, and shows you how to how to do the same things with tidyxl and unpivotr.

But even tidy tables can have hidden gotchas, such as meaningful formatting and sentinel values, so these are covered too.
